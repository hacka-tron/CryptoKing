What is CryptoKing?

Only the best WebApp Ever!!

The app is a crypto currency investing game that tests your investing knowledge(and luck). Go head to head against your friends and see who can grow their portfolios the fastest!

How Was This Made?

All Through Mean Development!

By watching several Angular/NodeJS/MongoDB tutorials, parts were pieced together to create different components of the app. Once a firm understanding was had over the technology, improvements started rolling in.
