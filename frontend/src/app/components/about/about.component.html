About

What is CryptoKing?

Only the best WebApp Ever!! The app is a cryptocurrency investing game that tests your investing knowledge (and luck). Go head to head against your friends and see who can grow their portfolios the fastest!

 How Was This Made?

All Through development with the MEAN stack! By watching several Angular/NodeJS/MongoDB tutorials, different parts of the app were pieced together. Once a firm understanding was had over the technology, improvements started rolling in.
